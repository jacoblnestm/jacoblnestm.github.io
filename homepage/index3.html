3rd place

The third best borough in NYC is Queens! Some people think Queens is the best, and they make a good point.
The reason I'm ranking this one lower is because Queens doesn't have as much interesting stuff as Manhattan and Brooklyn.
Although, to give it credit, it does have Rockaway Beach, the US Open, and great neighborhoods like Astoria and Jackson Heights.
Queens does also lose points because the Mets play here.

This landmark, the Unisphere, is a 120 ft tall stainless steel globe in Flushing Meadows Corona Park.
It's one of the biggest landmarks in Queens.
